Voice Similarity Assessment

Training Phase

The first three comparisons are training examples to familiarise yourself with the experiment. Each contains two short voice recordings. Rate the similarity of the voices on a scale from 1—9, where 1 indicates that they are very different, and 9 that they are very similar. Thus, a HIGHER number indicates a MORE SIMILAR voice. When assessing similarity, do not focus on the accents of the speakers, any non-speech noises, speaking style or any of the spoken content. Instead, aim to give your overall impression of how similar the voices sound.
