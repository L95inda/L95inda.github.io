Voice Similarity Assessment

Training Phase

The first three comparisons are training examples to familiarise yourself with the experiment.

Each contains two short voice recordings of male speakers. Rate the similarity of the voices on a scale from 1—9, where 1 indicates that they are very dissimilar, and 9 that they are very similar. Thus, a HIGHER score indicates a MORE SIMILAR voice.

When assessing similarity, try not to focus on the accents of the speakers, speaking style or any of the spoken content. Instead, aim to give your overall impression of how similar the voices sound. Please listen to each clip only once.
